Digital Communications

This section showcases my multidisciplinary skills in digital media through a series of applied communication projects. From prototype creation and content development to basic website programming, each piece reflects my ability to blend strategy, creativity, and functionality. I’ve worked with tools like Figma to design user-focused prototypes, crafted written and visual content for diverse audiences, and built responsive web pages using HTML and CSS. Additionally, I’ve applied core principles of usability and design to enhance user experience and created data-driven infographics that simplify complex information into engaging visual narratives. Together, these projects demonstrate my well-rounded approach to effective and impactful digital communication.

Key Learnings from Digital Communications

  • Using Figma taught me to focus on usability early in the design process. I learned how layout and flow impact user experience, shifting my approach from visual design to functional design.
  • Creating content helped me realize that strong messaging goes beyond writing—it requires understanding audience needs, platform strategy, and tone to drive real engagement.
  • HTML and CSS challenged me at first, but building pages from scratch helped me gain confidence in coding and reinforced the connection between structure, design, and user experience.
